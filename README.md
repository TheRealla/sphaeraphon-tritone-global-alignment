# Sphäraphon: Music of the Spheres for Tritone – Global Alignment Edition

A modern heterodyne instrument inspired by Jörg Mager’s 1924 booklet *Eine neue Epoche der Musik durch Radio*.

In 2026, as President Donald Trump calls on nations to help secure the Strait of Hormuz and restore global flow, this plugin treats the **tritone** as a metaphor for necessary tension that leads to alignment and harmony.

Just as countries must glide through friction to protect shared pathways, the performer approaches the augmented fourth (≈1.4142 ratio) with the brass crank — building cosmic tension until the filter boosts into a glassy “event,” then resolves into pure spherical resonance.

## Features
- Authentic 50 kHz heterodyne engine (fixed carrier + crank-controlled modulator)
- Real-time Tritone Cosmic Event: Q=20 bandpass + tube glow when alignment hits
- Aether Drift (0.2 Hz Perlin-style), Tube Heat saturation, Hollow/Pure speaker filter
- Expression pedal swells with 500 ms vacuum-tube smoothing
- Vast cosmic reverb (Size 90, 8s decay) for infinite vacuum trails

## Play the Alignment
- Hold expression steady.
- Slowly rotate the crank (Mod Wheel CC#1) from a perfect fifth toward the tritone.
- Feel the living drift, then the piercing metallic shriek of alignment.
- Resolve into hollow purity — a new epoch of music through global/radio-inspired cooperation.

Built for Amorph (Cmajor) and adaptable to JUCE/Max/MSP. GUI: 1924 physics lab with glowing celestial sphere and rotating brass crank.
